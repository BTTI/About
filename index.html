About Us

The BontoTTI Information and Communication Technology Department is committed to naturing young talent in Computing and imparting knowledge to learners, preparing them for the Job market and to make them responsible revolutionists.

Our Diploma program is designed to train information technologists for productive, life-long careers. Such education equips students with a sound knowledge of the foundations of computer science, and problem solving and system design skills necessary for designing and building robust, efficient, reliable, scalable, and flexible software systems. We also work in close ties with the Engineering and Business Departments to equip all our students in both Diploma and Certificate courses with a basic knowledge of computing.
